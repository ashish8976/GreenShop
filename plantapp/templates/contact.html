FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How many days does delivery take?
We usually deliver within 3–5 working days. In metro cities, delivery may arrive within 2–3 days. You’ll receive a tracking link as soon as your order is placed.
What if the plant arrives damaged?
No worries! Contact us within 48 hours of delivery and share a photo of the plant. We’ll provide a free replacement or full refund.
Are bulk orders available?
Yes, absolutely! We accept bulk orders for events, offices, and gifts. Contact us for special bulk discounts.
How do I take care of my plants?
Every plant comes with a detailed care card. You’ll also find watering, sunlight, and temperature details on the product page. If you still need help, we’re always available!
Is COD (Cash on Delivery) available?
Yes! We accept COD, UPI, credit/debit cards, and net banking. Choose the payment method that’s most convenient for you.
