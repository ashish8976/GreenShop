About Us — The Story of GreenShop

GreenShop started with one simple idea — every home should have at least one plant. We want people to bring a little greenery and happiness into their homes, offices, and lives.

We offer more than 500+ plant varieties — indoor, outdoor, succulents, flowering plants — all in one place. Every plant comes with an expert care guide so your plant always stays healthy.

We don’t just sell plants — we promote a greener lifestyle. 🌱
